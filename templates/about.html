About Us

Welcome to EduLink

EduLink is an innovative web app designed for students and quiz enthusiasts. Join us to test your knowledge, explore new subjects, and enjoy a variety of engaging quizzes.

How It Works

EduLink provides a user-friendly interface that allows you to:

  • Create a personalized account to access exclusive content.
  • Choose from a wide range of quiz topics tailored for academic and fun challenges.
  • Participate in timed quizzes and answer questions to boost your ranking.
  • Monitor your progress and compete with peers on our leaderboard.
